Ondia Logan

Ondia Logan is a rising sophomore at the University at Albany. She is a Girls Beyond Senior Ambassador. This role allows her to further develop her leadership skills by leading discussions and presentations; leading a committee and bringing innovative ideas to the group. She loves being in Girls Beyond because it exposes her to many different opportunities, which allow her to expand her network. While at Albany High School, apart from her involvement with Girls Beyond, Ondia was involved in the Best Buddies organization to create opportunities for students with intellectual and developmental disabilities as well as a clarinetist in the symphonic band. Her hobbies include community service and engaging with the youth, as she aspires to become an educator. Her favorite quote is “I am no longer accepting the things I cannot change. I am changing the things I cannot accept.” -Angela Davis.
